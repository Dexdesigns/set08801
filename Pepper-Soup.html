How to Nigerian Pepper Soup

Ingredient

  • 1 lb of assorted meat (goat meat, beef, chicken, or fish)
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tablespoon of dried thyme
  • 1 tablespoon of ground cumin
  • 1 tablespoon of ground coriander
  • 2 scotch bonnet peppers (habanero peppers), chopped
  • 1 teaspoon of ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon of ground white pepper
  • 2 cubes of Maggi seasoning (or any other bouillon cube)
  • 4 cups of water
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  • Clean the meat and set aside.
  • Heat a large pot over medium heat.
  • Add the chopped onion and sauté for 2-3 minutes until translucent.
  • Add the minced garlic and sauté for another minute.
  • Add the meat to the pot and sauté until browned on all sides.
  • Add the dried thyme, ground cumin, ground coriander, scotch bonnet peppers, ground black pepper, and ground white pepper, and stir to combine.
  • Add the Maggi seasoning cubes and water to the pot, and stir to combine.
  • Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat to low and simmer for 45 minutes to 1 hour until the meat is tender.
  • Add salt to taste, and adjust any other seasonings as desired.
  • Remove from heat and serve hot with your favorite side dish.
