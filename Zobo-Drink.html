How to Cook Nigerian Jollof Rice

Ingredient

  • 2 cups of dried hibiscus flowers (Zobo leaves)
  • 2 liters of water
  • 1 cup of sugar or honey
  • 1 ginger root, peeled and grated
  • 2 cinnamon sticks (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon of cloves (optional)
  • 1 orange or lemon, sliced (optional)

Instructions

  • Rinse the hibiscus flowers thoroughly with cold water to remove any dirt or debris.
  • Place the hibiscus flowers in a pot and add 2 liters of water.
  • Add the grated ginger, cinnamon sticks, cloves, and orange or lemon slices (if using).
  • Bring the mixture to a boil and then reduce the heat to low. Allow the mixture to simmer for about 20-25 minutes.
  • Remove the pot from the heat and strain the mixture using a fine-mesh sieve or cheesecloth.
  • Add sugar or honey to taste and stir to dissolve.
  • Allow the mixture to cool to room temperature, then transfer to a pitcher and refrigerate for at least 1 hour or until chilled.
  • Serve the Zobo drink over ice and garnish with fresh mint leaves or sliced citrus fruits, if desired.
